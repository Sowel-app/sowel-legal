Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Sowel ("we", "us", "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: email address, display name (Soul Name), profile photo
  • Content you create: posts, comments, ideas, mood diary entries, messages
  • Activity data: likes, saves, follows, session requests
  • Professional information: for therapist verification — diplomas, certificates, specialty
  • Device information: device type, OS version (collected by Firebase automatically)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Sowel service
  • To personalize your experience
  • To enable communication between users
  • To verify professional therapist credentials
  • To moderate content and ensure community safety
  • To send notifications about activity relevant to you
  • To improve our app and develop new features

4. Information We Share

We do not sell your personal data. We share data with:

  • Firebase (Google): for authentication, database, and storage services
  • Cloudinary: for image and file storage (profile photos, post images, certificates)
  • Other users: your public profile, posts, and ideas are visible to other Sowel users according to your privacy settings

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all your personal data is permanently removed within 30 days, except:

  • Anonymized reports submitted for moderation purposes
  • Aggregate analytics data (no personal identifiers)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at sowelapp@gmail.com or delete your account directly in the app via Settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Sowel is intended for users aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we become aware that a child under 16 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption for all data transmission and Firebase security rules to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Third-Party Links

Sowel may contain links to external websites (e.g. therapist registry links, Ko-fi donation pages). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of Sowel after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
