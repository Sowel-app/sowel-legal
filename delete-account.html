Account Deletion

Delete Your Sowel Account

You can request the deletion of your Sowel account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how to do it and what data will be removed.

How to Delete Your Account

1
Open the Sowel app on your Android device
2
Go to your Profile → tap the ⚙️ Settings icon
3
Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
4
Confirm deletion — your account and data will be permanently removed

⚠️ If you cannot access the app, you can also request deletion by contacting us at the email below.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile (Soul Name, bio, avatar)
  • All posts, motivations, and ideas you published
  • Your mood diary entries
  • Your chat messages
  • Your Sowels balance and transaction history
  • Your saved posts and ideas
  • Your follower/following connections

What Data May Be Retained

Some data may be retained for legal or security reasons:

  • Reports you submitted (kept for moderation purposes, anonymized)
  • Aggregate analytics (no personal identifiers)

Retained data is fully anonymized and cannot be linked back to you.

Partial Data Deletion

You can also delete specific data without deleting your full account:

  • Delete individual posts or comments from within the app
  • Clear your mood diary entries
  • Remove saved ideas or posts
